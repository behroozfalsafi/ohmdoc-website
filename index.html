The Problems Every Testing Shop Deals With

OhmDoc was built by a technician who lived every one of these problems.

📦 Inventory is a black box
Nobody knows exactly what's in the yard — how many units, what type, what status. Work gets duplicated, units get missed.
Real-time inventory dashboard with every unit, every status, searchable in seconds.
📁 Test sheets get lost. Reports go missing.
Paper forms scatter. Digital files end up in the wrong folder or never get filed at all.
Every test sheet and report lives in OhmDoc permanently. One search, one tap, it's there.
🔁 The same data gets entered three times
One tech fills out a report. Another fills out theirs. A clerk re-enters it all from scratch. Same serial number, typed over and over.
Unit data captured once at intake. Every report and document pulls from the same record automatically.
⏳ Senior techs spend hours approving paperwork
A lead tech has to sit down, pull physical sheets, manually review every value, and sign off one by one.
Pending approvals in a queue. Review results, compare to history, approve with one tap.
📅 Expired calibration standards slip through
A tech runs a test with an out-of-cal standard. Nobody catches it — now the results are invalid and the test has to be repeated.
Every standard tracked with expiration status visible at a glance. Always know what's current.
🔍 Unit history is scattered or nonexistent
A transformer comes back to the shop. Was this the same problem as last time? Nobody knows because the records are gone.
Complete asset history for every unit — every test, every technician, every status, every return.
🧮 Manual calculations introduce errors
Techs do unit conversions by hand, use the wrong multiplier, or enter values in the wrong unit. The error makes it into the report.
Configured units, multipliers, and limits. Calculated fields update automatically. Out-of-range values flagged immediately.
❓ Junior techs don't know what tests to run
An unfamiliar unit comes in. The tech calls a senior who gives their best guess from memory. The procedure lives in someone's head.
AI assistant trained on your shop's procedures tells techs what to run, what standards to use, what instruments to reach for.
📵 Supervisors are unreachable when decisions need to be made
A unit fails and needs to go RTM. The supervisor is off-site. Nothing moves until they're back.
Supervisors get full visibility from their phone — unit status, test results, RTM flags — decisions made same day.
🏷️ Labels are created manually and full of errors
Someone types the serial number by hand. One transposed digit and the unit is misidentified for its entire life in the system.
OhmDoc generates unit labels automatically from scanned data. One tap, correct label, every time.

What OhmDoc Does

Everything Your Shop Needs.
Nothing It Doesn't.

Fully customizable for any equipment type. Works with transformers, switchgear, motors, and anything else your shop tests.

📷
Quick Scan Intake
Scan the barcode on any unit label. OhmDoc pulls the serial number, manufacturer, and material code automatically. The unit is added to inventory and ready for testing — no manual entry. Ideal for SAP-based operations with standardized material codes.
📋
Fully Customizable Test Sheets
Build test sheets from scratch using any combination of field types — text, numbers, calculations, photos, signatures, and more. Configure it once for each equipment type and reuse it forever. No hardcoded assumptions about what you test.
𝑓(𝑥)
Built-In Formula Engine
Define calculated fields that compute automatically from your test data. Temperature correction, polarization index, turns ratio expected values, pass/fail logic — all configurable without external tools. Fields flag out-of-range values instantly.
📦
Inventory & Asset History
Every unit carries a full asset history — when it arrived, who tested it, who approved it, and every status including pass, fail, and return to manufacturer. If a unit comes back, the as-found status is already on record.
📊
Analytics & Manufacturer Intelligence
Track pass rates by manufacturer, model, equipment type, and technician automatically. Set testing goals with deadlines and monitor real-time progress. Build institutional knowledge about which vendors deliver reliable equipment.
🛡️
Calibration Standards Tracking
Maintain a complete list of calibration standards and reference instruments with expiration status visible at a glance. Always know what's current before a test begins. No spreadsheets, no wall calendars, no surprises.
📄
Professional PDF Reports
When a test is complete, OhmDoc generates a clean, structured PDF report — formatted, signed, and ready to share. No reformatting. No manual entry. The report is a direct output of the test data your technician entered.
✅
One-Tap Approval Workflow
Pending test sheets queue up for supervisor review. Open one, review results, compare to historical data if needed, and approve with a single tap. No stacks of paper. No hunting for files. Hours of approval work reduced to minutes.
🤖
Optional AI Assistant
An AI assistant that learns your shop over time. Ask what tests to run, get guidance on procedures, or describe a calculation in plain language and let AI write the formula syntax for you. Gets smarter and more relevant the more your team uses it.
🏷️
Tags & Cohort Tracking
Group units and test sheets however your operation requires. Track inspection vs. reconditioning ratios against targets. Follow a specific batch of equipment — like a pilot program — from intake through final disposition.
🔐
Role-Based Access Control
Every team member gets exactly the access they need. Technicians see their work queue. Supervisors see approvals and analytics. Admins control everything. Guest access for visitors with zero data exposure.
☁️
Flexible Data Storage
Choose where your data lives. On-device only for maximum privacy, your own company server for full control, or OhmDoc's encrypted cloud for seamless multi-user sync. Your data, your rules.

OhmDoc is growing from a field testing tool into a full shop management platform. Here's what's next.

🧠
AI-Assisted Material Code Setup
The AI will read equipment nameplates and populate your material code table automatically — KVA, voltage class, manufacturer, type — with no manual column entry.

AI Formula Building
Describe a calculation in plain language — "divide KVA by primary voltage multiplied by three" — and the AI translates it into the correct formula syntax instantly.
🌐
Web Dashboard for Admins
Full inventory, analytics, and report approval access from any browser — no app install required. Built for supervisors and managers who need visibility without being on the floor.
📸
Point. Scan. Start Testing.
Photograph any nameplate and OhmDoc identifies the unit, pulls the right test sheet, and flags any missing instruments — before testing begins.
📡
Bluetooth Instrument Integration
Connect Bluetooth-capable test instruments directly to OhmDoc. Readings push into the test sheet automatically — no manual transcription, no errors.
📝
Smart Document Generation
One-click RTM letters, failure notifications, and RCA reports. Auto-filled with unit data, test results, and manufacturer contacts from your database. Preview, edit, send.

Test Connection Diagrams
Embed wiring diagrams directly inside test sheets so technicians know exactly how to connect instruments before they start — insulation resistance, TTR, power factor, and more.
📈
AI-Generated Reports
Monthly analytics and inventory summaries generated automatically by the AI — pass rate trends, manufacturer performance, testing goals — formatted and ready to share with management.
👁️
AI Visual Inspection
Attach photos or video during internal inspections. OhmDoc flags visual anomalies against a library of known failure patterns — loose hardware, metal contamination, insulation damage.
